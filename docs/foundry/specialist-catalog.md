# Specialist Catalog

This catalog defines operational specialist archetypes that the Foundry may assemble into governed mission topologies.

## Researcher

Responsibilities:
- evidence gathering
- source validation
- domain mapping
- uncertainty exposure

## Strategist

Responsibilities:
- objective planning
- resource allocation
- operational sequencing
- long-range coordination

## Architect

Responsibilities:
- systems design
- topology planning
- scalability analysis
- dependency management

## Builder

Responsibilities:
- implementation
- artifact production
- operational execution
- runtime adaptation

## Auditor

Responsibilities:
- verification-of-verification
- evidence-chain reconstruction
- compliance auditing
- trust validation

## Blackquill Review Authorities

Blackquill authorities are attached as oversight gates, not ordinary worker agents.

Examples:
- Blackquill Architect
- Blackquill Compliance
- Blackquill Strategos
- Blackquill SecOps
- Blackquill Researcher

Responsibilities:
- contradiction exposure
- governance pressure
- structural review
- repair-condition issuance
- drift detection

## Orchestrator

Responsibilities:
- specialist coordination
- operational sequencing
- escalation routing
- topology management
- artifact continuity

## Persona Factory

Persona Factory is responsible for:
- profession inference
- specialist assembly
- operational staffing
- mission graph generation

Persona Factory is the synthesis engine of the Foundry.
